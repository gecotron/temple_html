Temple html!

Welcome to temple_html, a short interactive text game, based on the temples of ancient Egypt!

Are you ready to step into the shoes of a priest of one of these temples, to learn and navigate its rooms to fufill your duties? Press on ahead, but be sure to read the guide below.

Valid commands

  • /east/south/west - This allows you to move around the temple. You can add an additional value to make quick strides through the long hallway and large courtyard!
  • ritual [god/place] [object/action] - The most important thing you can do as a priest! Be sure to reference the room with look and your items with inventory to get clues on what to do, and that you can perform the task.
  • look - When a glance at the room isn't enough, you can look around for a better description of the room, as well as some hints for rituals and items.
  • map/area - While you should have your trusty paper map on you to help you navigate, you can get a feel of the nearby rooms and area with this.
  • inventory/stuff - Check your pockets for what you have on you. Useful to make sure you grabbed the offering you needed for a ritual.
  • get/grab - Pick up an item in a room. Do note that picking up items after performing rituals with them RESET THE RITUAL.
  • drop/place - Put down an object from your inventory. Required for some rituals as the item needs to be interacted with, and not used.

Remember, you can reference this again with the help command while you play!
